About Kaladharaa

Kaladharaa Dance is an established dance organization based in Utah & Washington D.C. (USA) who’s mission is to promote and preserve Indian Classical Dance & Music across boundaries, focusing mainly on ‘Kathak’ which is one amongst the eight Indian classical dance forms, with its origin from North India. Nidhika Loomba & Sonali Loomba are the artistic directors of this organization who have studied Kathak extensively under the guidance of their Guru’s Sri Hari & Smt. Chethana Hari, Internationally recognized Kathak dance couple from Bangalore, India.

Kaladharaa Dance operates under a nonprofit organization, Samarpan Art Center, a 501(c)(3) who also support welfare organizations,through art.
